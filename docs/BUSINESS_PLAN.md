# Business Plan: DevOps Game Night

## Vision
To provide a stress-free, engaging environment for engineers to practice DevOps skills through daily gamified challenges.

## Audience
- Junior to Mid-level DevOps Engineers.
- Software Engineers wanting to learn Ops.

## MVP Scope
1.  **User Profiles**: Track streaks and progress.
2.  **Daily Challenges**: One small DevOps task per day.
3.  **AI Coach**: Personalized feedback and encouragement.
4.  **Activity Log**: Record of completed tasks.

## KPIs
1.  **Daily Active Users (DAU)**: Target 100 in month 1.
2.  **Completion Rate**: > 60% of started challenges.
3.  **Streak Retention**: > 20% of users maintain a 7-day streak.

## Change Policy
- **Versioning**: Semantic Versioning (Major.Minor.Patch).
- **API**: V1 is stable. V2 introduced for breaking changes.
- **Database**: Expand-Migrate-Cleanup pattern for zero-downtime migrations.
